Medical imaging & perception.

Vision systems that help clinicians read medical scans, surfacing what is subtle, time-sensitive, or easily missed under workload.

The work.

Sysqo builds perception systems for CT, MRI, X-ray, and pathology slides, trained to detect and triage findings that are faint, urgent, or easy to overlook at the end of a long list.

These systems support clinicians. A radiologist makes every diagnosis and holds every decision. The system offers a second pair of eyes, flagging and prioritising findings for the expert to review.

What we build.

01Detection & segmentation in radiologyLocating and outlining abnormalities across modalities, drawing the clinician's eye to a region and quantifying its extent.
02Pathology slide analysisWorking through gigapixel whole-slide images to surface regions a pathologist may want to examine first.
03Triage & prioritisationRe-ordering the queue so the scan that cannot wait is read first, moving urgent findings to the front.
04Quantification over timeMeasuring how a lesion changes between studies, turning a subjective impression of progression into a consistent, comparable number.
05Quality & consistency checksCatching protocol deviations and image-quality issues at acquisition, before they reach the read.

Why it matters.

urgent first

fewer missed
Support aimed at reducing false negatives

Early detection is also longevity work. Catching disease sooner is one of the most direct ways to extend healthy life.

Engaged by a leading medical centre to provide detection and triage support in the radiology workflow, prioritising urgent studies for clinicians who remain the decision-makers on every read.
